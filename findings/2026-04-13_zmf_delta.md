# Funding-Delta Probe — 2026-04-13 (null; level edge re-confirmed)

Test: does the 8h change in mean BitMEX+Kraken funding (`delta_mf`) carry information beyond the level (`z_mf`) for BTC 24h forward returns?

## Pre-registered

- Same venue set and 8h grid as `dispersion_narrow`.
- Model A: `fwd_24h ~ z_delta_mf` (delta alone).
- Model B: `fwd_24h ~ z_mf + z_delta_mf` (does delta add?).
- IS = first 70% of 8h timestamps (n=770), OOS = last 30% (n=331).
- Reject null on delta: OOS |t|(β_delta in Model B) ≥ 1.96 AND sign consistent IS→OOS.

## Results (Model B)

| Split | β(z_mf) | t | β(z_delta) | t |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| IS  | −0.00183 | −2.27 | +0.00135 | +1.68 |
| OOS | −0.00256 | −2.03 | +0.00147 | +1.07 |

Model A (delta alone) OOS: t(z_delta)=+0.11. Delta carries no signal alone.

## Interpretation

- Delta t-stats: IS +1.68, OOS +1.07. Same sign, below 1.96 threshold. Not an independent edge.
- Level signal re-confirms: OOS β(z_mf)=−0.00256, t=−2.03. This is the third time the mean-funding reversal has printed negative-and-significant OOS at the 24h horizon (dispersion_narrow, ETH cross-asset weakly, and here). **Three independent regressions with different covariates, same OOS sign and magnitude — this is the strongest we have.**
- Positive delta sign (if real) would mean: funding *rising* over the last 8h predicts slightly higher 24h return, independent of the level. That is mildly counter-mean-reverting, plausibly a momentum kernel within the broader reversion. Not actionable at this n.

## Decision

Delta is not an independent edge. Do not carry forward. The z_mf level result is now re-validated across three specifications and graduates from "one OOS replication" to "consistent OOS replication across three model specifications" — a stronger-than-usual result at this research site. Should anchor the eventual Phase D1 portfolio work.
